About me

From a profesional to hybrid profesional

Studying at the University of Zagreb was the best part of my life where I received the degree of an Engineer of Biotechnology. After more than 10 years working for the Ministry of Environment Protection and the Ministry of Agriculture, I decided to enrich my experience in the sector of IT technology that was always present and waiting for me to catch it. My wish came true two years ago in Barcelona where I started my studies at the IT Academy of Barcelona activa as a junior front-end developer. Studying and practicing at the same time is my daily rutine that makes me feel very proactive and never bored.

I have spent more than a year learning, improving and practicing in the world of web development, following itinerary of the IT Academy as a part of my neverending life academy.....
